Naan Pizza

Description

Pizza night has never been easier! 10-minute Naan Pizza that has a golden crispy crust, tons of cheese, and is so yummy and quick. Your family will love this simple recipe that takes no time at all to make!

Ingredients

  • 4 naan bread pieces
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • Optional toppings- basil, red pepper flakes, pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Place naans on covered baking sheets
  3. Top with sauce, shredded cheese
  4. Bake pizzas for about 10 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes. Garnish, slice, and serve
