Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken katsu is juicy, crispy, and comforting. It only takes 25 minutes and 7 ingredients to make this easy chicken dinner that just might be better than what your local Japanese restaurant sells.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb thinly sliced chicken breast
  • 1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/3 cup mayo
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Season chicken, rest up to overnight
  2. Turn stove to high, heat oil in skillet
  3. Put chicken in bag with mayo
  4. Toss chicken in plate of breadcrumbs
  5. Cook chicken in skillet, around 4 minutes each side
  6. Place chicken on cooling rack, add salt to taste
  7. Slice, and serve with tonkatsu sauce, rice, and cabbage
